Total: ~500+ practice questions across all interview types.

## Confidence Levels

- **High:** Rich data from multiple sources (Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind, Aakash Gupta content). Tier 1 and Tier 2 companies.
- **Medium:** Solid data from Glassdoor and industry knowledge. Most Tier 3-6 companies.
- **Low-Medium:** Limited public data. Profiles are based on industry analysis and general patterns. Always supplement with fresh Glassdoor research.

## How to Use This Data

1. **Before applying:** Read the company profile to understand culture and what they value
2. **Before interviewing:** Study the question bank in the company profile AND the relevant sections of behavioral-interview-frameworks.md for frameworks
3. **Before negotiating:** Check the compensation data and cross-reference with levels.fyi
4. **After interviewing:** Update the profile with your own experience to help future you (or share with others)

### Recommended Preparation Order
1. Master the Three Laws of Answer Grading and the 200-Word Rule (behavioral-interview-frameworks.md)
2. Write and practice answers to the 12 Most Common Behavioral Questions
3. Study the target company profile in company-intel/
4. Practice the relevant interview type frameworks (CIRCLES for design, GAME for metrics, etc.)
5. Run 3-5 mock interviews before the real thing

## Keeping This Current

Interview processes change. Compensation shifts. Companies reorganize. To keep this database valuable:

1. Always check Glassdoor 1-2 weeks before your interview for the latest data
2. Verify comp ranges on levels.fyi (they update in real-time)
3. Add your own interview experience after each interview
4. Update confidence levels as you gather more data
