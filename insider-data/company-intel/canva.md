# Canva - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, industry reports
Confidence: Medium

## Company Overview
- Stage: Late-stage private (valued at ~$26B as of 2024)
- PM team size estimate: 100-200 PMs across Design, Video, Print, Enterprise, and AI
- PM comp range: Senior PM $200K-$350K TC | Staff PM $300K-$480K TC (Sydney-based comp may differ)
- Culture notes: Australian-founded (Sydney HQ), design-democratization mission. Collaborative, inclusive culture with strong values. "Empowering the world to design." Rapid growth from prosumer to enterprise.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Round 0: Recruiter screen - 30 min
- Round 1: Hiring Manager screen - 45-60 min
- Round 2: Product case - 60 min
- Round 3: Values / culture - 45 min
- Round 4: Leadership - 30-45 min

## Question Bank
### Product Sense / Design
- "How would you improve Canva for enterprise teams?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design an AI feature for Canva." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you expand Canva into a new vertical (e.g., video)?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Execution / Metrics
- "What metrics matter for Canva?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you measure the success of a new template category?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

## What They Screen For
- Design accessibility passion: making design tools for everyone (source: Glassdoor)
- Product-led growth understanding (source: Glassdoor)
- Values alignment: Canva has strong cultural values (source: Glassdoor)
- AI integration thinking (source: Glassdoor)

## Tips
- Canva competes with Adobe, Figma, and AI design tools. Know the competitive landscape. (source: Glassdoor)
- Understand the freemium-to-enterprise journey. Canva is in the midst of this transition. (source: Glassdoor)
- Sydney time zone may be relevant for interviews. (source: Glassdoor)
- Use Canva before interviewing. Understand both free and Pro features. (source: Glassdoor)

## Compensation Data
- Senior PM: $200K-$350K TC (source: Glassdoor estimates, varies by location)
- Staff PM: $300K-$480K TC (source: Glassdoor estimates)
- Note: Sydney-based roles may have different comp structures. AUD vs USD considerations.
