# Cloudflare - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium-High

## Company Overview
- Stage: Public (NET)
- PM team size estimate: 100-200 PMs across Network Services, Security, Developer Platform (Workers), and AI
- PM comp range: Senior PM $240K-$380K TC | Staff PM $350K-$520K TC | Director $480K-$700K+ TC
- Culture notes: Infrastructure and security company with developer platform ambitions. Matthew Prince (CEO) is highly visible and product-involved. Innovation-driven with "Birthday Week" launches. Strong engineering culture.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, technical deep-dive, behavioral/leadership
