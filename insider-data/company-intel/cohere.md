# Cohere - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, Blind, industry reports
Confidence: Medium

## Company Overview
- Stage: Late-stage private (valued at ~$5.5B as of 2025)
- PM team size estimate: 20-40 PMs across API Platform, Enterprise, and Core Models
- PM comp range: Senior PM $250K-$400K TC | Staff PM $350K-$550K TC (with equity)
- Culture notes: Enterprise-focused AI company founded by ex-Google researchers (Aidan Gomesz, co-author of "Attention Is All You Need"). Based in Toronto. Focused on enterprise AI and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). More B2B-oriented than OpenAI/Anthropic.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks (startup pace)
- Round 0: Recruiter screen - 30 min
- Round 1: Hiring Manager screen - 45-60 min
- Round 2: Product case - PM - 60 min
- Round 3: Technical / AI depth - Engineer or Researcher - 45-60 min
- Round 4: Culture / leadership - 45 min

## Question Bank
### Behavioral
- "Why enterprise AI? Why Cohere specifically?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Tell me about a time you built a developer-facing product." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Product Sense / Design
- "Design an enterprise search product using LLMs." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you improve Cohere's API developer experience?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design a RAG-powered product for a specific industry." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Execution / Metrics
- "How would you measure the success of an enterprise AI API?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "What metrics matter for a developer platform?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Technical
- "Explain RAG and when to use it vs. fine-tuning." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "What are embeddings and how are they used in search?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How does Cohere's approach differ from OpenAI?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

## What They Screen For
- Enterprise AI understanding: B2B AI product sense (source: Glassdoor)
- Technical depth in NLP/LLMs: must understand embeddings, RAG, fine-tuning (source: Glassdoor)
- Developer empathy: building for developers and ML engineers (source: Glassdoor)

## Tips
- Cohere differentiates on enterprise focus, data privacy, and deployability (on-prem, cloud-agnostic). Know these differentiators. (source: Glassdoor)
- Understand RAG deeply -- it's central to Cohere's product strategy. (source: Glassdoor)
- Toronto-based with remote options. Understand the Canadian tech ecosystem. (source: Glassdoor)

## Compensation Data
- Senior PM: $250K-$400K TC (source: Glassdoor estimates)
- Staff PM: $350K-$550K TC (source: Glassdoor estimates)
