# Coinbase - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium-High

## Company Overview
- Stage: Public (COIN)
- PM team size estimate: 100-200 PMs across Consumer, Institutional, Base (L2), Wallet, and Commerce
- PM comp range: L4 $220K-$360K TC | L5 $320K-$500K TC | L6 $450K-$700K+ TC
- Culture notes: Crypto-native culture. Remote-first. "Mission-focused" approach with clear communication standards. PMs must understand blockchain and crypto markets. Volatile business tied to crypto cycles.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Round 0: Recruiter screen - 30 min
- Round 1: Hiring Manager screen - 45 min
- Round 2: Product case - 60 min
- Round 3: Analytical / execution - 45-60 min
- Round 4: Behavioral / culture - 45 min

## What They Screen For
- Crypto/blockchain knowledge: must understand the space (source: Glassdoor)
- Regulatory awareness: crypto is heavily regulated (source: Glassdoor)
- Product craft in volatile markets: building for boom and bust cycles (source: Glassdoor)
