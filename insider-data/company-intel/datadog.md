# Datadog - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium-High

## Company Overview
- Stage: Public (DDOG)
- PM team size estimate: 100-200 PMs across Infrastructure Monitoring, APM, Logs, Security, and AI
- PM comp range: Senior PM $240K-$400K TC | Staff PM $360K-$550K TC | Director $500K-$700K+ TC
- Culture notes: High-performance, execution-focused culture. Olivier Pomel (CEO) is deeply technical. Strong engineering culture with emphasis on product quality. NYC-based.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, technical, behavioral/leadership

## Question Bank
### Product Sense / Design
- "How would you improve Datadog's dashboard experience?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design a product for Datadog to help with AI/ML observability." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you improve Datadog's onboarding?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Technical
- "Explain how application monitoring works." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "What are the challenges of collecting and processing telemetry data at scale?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

## What They Screen For
- DevOps/infrastructure knowledge (source: Glassdoor)
- Technical depth in observability (source: Glassdoor)
- Developer empathy (source: Glassdoor)

- AI/ML observability is a growth area. (source: Glassdoor)
