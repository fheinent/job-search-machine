# Discord - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium

## Company Overview
- Stage: Late-stage private (valued at ~$15B as of 2021, likely lower in 2024-25)
- PM team size estimate: 60-100 PMs across Core Product, Growth, Monetization, Safety, and Platform
- PM comp range: Senior PM $240K-$380K TC | Staff PM $350K-$520K TC
- Culture notes: Community-first culture. Originally gaming-focused, now broader. SF-based. Young, energetic team. Strong emphasis on community building and user-centric design.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, analytical, culture fit

## Question Bank
### Product Sense / Design
- "How would you improve Discord for non-gaming communities?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design a monetization feature for Discord servers." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you improve Discord's discovery experience?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Execution / Metrics
- "What metrics matter for a community platform?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

## What They Screen For
- Community/social product sense (source: Glassdoor)
- Gaming/youth culture awareness (source: Glassdoor)
- Monetization creativity (source: Glassdoor)
- Safety/trust product thinking (source: Glassdoor)

## Tips
- Discord's challenge is monetization. Show you understand the tension between free community value and revenue. (source: Glassdoor)
- Know the competitive landscape: Slack, Telegram, Guilded. (source: Glassdoor)
- Use Discord actively before interviewing. (source: Glassdoor)
