## Company Overview
- Stage: Late-stage private (~$9.5B)
- Product focus: Payroll and HR for SMBs
- PM team size estimate: 50-80
- PM comp range: Senior PM $220K-$370K TC / Staff PM $330K-$500K TC
- Culture notes: Payroll, benefits, and HR for small businesses. SF-based. Mission-driven culture. Josh Reeves (CEO) is people-oriented.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, analytical/execution, behavioral/leadership
