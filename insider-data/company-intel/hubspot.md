# HubSpot - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium

## Company Overview
- Stage: Public (HUBS)
- Product focus: CRM and marketing platform
- PM team size estimate: 200-350
- PM comp range: Senior PM $200K-$340K TC, Staff PM $300K-$480K TC
- Culture notes: Inbound marketing pioneer. SMB-focused CRM competing with Salesforce. Known for great culture and work-life balance. Cambridge MA HQ.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, technical/analytical, behavioral/leadership

- "How would you improve HubSpot for scaling startups?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design an AI-powered marketing assistant for HubSpot." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Execution / Metrics

- SMB product sense, marketing/sales domain knowledge, PLG understanding
