# Company Intel Database - Index
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Total companies: 250

This database contains PM interview intelligence for 250 companies organized by tier. Each profile includes interview format, question banks, screening criteria, tips, and compensation data.

**How to use this database:**
1. Find your target company below
2. Open the corresponding .md file for detailed interview intel
3. Profiles marked "High confidence" have the richest data; "Low-Medium" profiles should be supplemented with Glassdoor research closer to your interview
4. Comp ranges are estimates -- always verify with levels.fyi and negotiate based on your specific situation

**Data freshness:** This database was built on the date above. Interview processes, comp ranges, and team structures change. Before any interview, run `/company-research [company]` to supplement with current web data. The pre-loaded intel gives you the structural patterns (interview format, question types, what they screen for) which change slowly. Specific details (open roles, recent launches, team size) should always be refreshed.

**Company not in the database?** Run `/company-research [company]` to generate a full research brief from the web. The skill produces the same structured output as these profiles.

**Variant files:** Some companies have additional specialized profiles:
- `[company]-ml.md` — ML/AI-specific interview intel (ML system design, research presentations)
- `[company]-related.md` — Related companies or subsidiaries with distinct interview processes
- Variant files supplement the main profile. Read both before interviewing.
