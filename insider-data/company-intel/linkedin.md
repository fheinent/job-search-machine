# LinkedIn (Microsoft) - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium

## Company Overview
- Stage: Microsoft subsidiary
- Product focus: Professional networking
- PM team size estimate: 200-400
- PM comp range: Senior PM $250K-$400K TC / Staff PM $370K-$560K TC
- Culture notes: World's largest professional network. Part of Microsoft. Strong data advantages. B2B and B2C hybrid. LinkedIn is increasingly a content platform.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, analytical/execution, behavioral/leadership
