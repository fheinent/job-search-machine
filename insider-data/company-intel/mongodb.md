# MongoDB - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium

## Company Overview
- Stage: Public (MDB)
- PM team size estimate: 80-150 PMs across Atlas, Server, Search, AI/Vector, and Developer Tools
- PM comp range: Senior PM $220K-$370K TC | Staff PM $330K-$500K TC | Director $450K-$650K+ TC
- Culture notes: Developer-focused database company. "Make data stunningly easy to work with." Strong community and developer advocacy. NYC-based.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, technical, behavioral
