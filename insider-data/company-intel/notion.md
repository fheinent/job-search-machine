# Notion - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium-High

## Company Overview
- Stage: Late-stage private (valued at ~$10B as of 2024)
- PM team size estimate: 40-80 PMs across Core Product, AI, Enterprise, and Growth
- PM comp range: L4 $240K-$370K TC | L5 $340K-$500K TC | L6 $460K-$680K+ TC
- Culture notes: Product-obsessed, craft-focused culture. "Make toolmaking accessible to everyone." PMs are expected to use Notion extensively and understand productivity workflows. Design-sensitive. SF-based.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Round 0: Recruiter screen - 30 min
- Round 1: Hiring Manager screen - 45-60 min
- Round 2: Product case - 60 min
- Round 3: Analytical / execution - 45-60 min
- Round 4: Culture / values - 45 min
