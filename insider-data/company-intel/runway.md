# Runway - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, industry reports
Confidence: Medium

## Company Overview
- Stage: Growth-stage private (valued at ~$4B as of 2024)
- PM team size estimate: 10-20 PMs across Video Generation, Creative Tools, and Enterprise
- PM comp range: Senior PM $220K-$380K TC | Staff PM $340K-$500K TC
- Culture notes: AI-native creative tools company. Pioneers of generative video (Gen-1, Gen-2, Gen-3). New York-based. Art and technology DNA. Small team with high creative standards.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 3-4
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks
- Round 1: Hiring Manager screen - 45 min
- Round 2: Product case / portfolio review - 60 min
- Round 3: Technical / culture - 45-60 min
- Round 4: Leadership - 30-45 min

## Question Bank
### Product Sense / Design
- "How would you design the next generation of AI video editing tools?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design a product for Runway to serve Hollywood studios." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you improve Runway's creative workflow for indie filmmakers?" (frequency: occasional, source: Glassdoor)

### Technical
- "How do generative video models work?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "What are the quality/speed trade-offs in video generation?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

## What They Screen For
- Creative + technical intersection: understanding both art and AI (source: Glassdoor)
- Video/media industry knowledge (source: Glassdoor)
- AI-native product thinking (source: Glassdoor)
- Startup scrappiness (source: Glassdoor)

## Tips
- Runway is at the intersection of AI and filmmaking. Show genuine interest in creative applications of AI. (source: Glassdoor)
- Know the competitive landscape: Sora (OpenAI), Pika, Kling, and Adobe Premiere AI features. (source: Glassdoor)
- Use Runway's products before interviewing. Hands-on experience is expected. (source: Glassdoor)

## Compensation Data
- Senior PM: $220K-$380K TC (source: Glassdoor estimates)
- Staff PM: $340K-$500K TC (source: Glassdoor estimates)
