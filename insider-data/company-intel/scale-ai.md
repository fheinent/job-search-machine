# Scale AI - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, Blind, industry reports
Confidence: Medium

## Company Overview
- Stage: Late-stage private (valued at ~$14B+ as of 2025)
- PM team size estimate: 50-100 PMs across Data Platform, Government/Defense, Generative AI, and Enterprise
- PM comp range: Senior PM $250K-$420K TC | Staff PM $380K-$580K TC
- Culture notes: Data infrastructure for AI. Founded by Alexandr Wang (youngest self-made billionaire). Aggressive growth culture with government/defense contracts. PMs bridge technical AI infrastructure with business needs.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks
- Round 0: Recruiter screen - 30 min
- Round 1: Hiring Manager screen - 45-60 min
- Round 2: Product case - 60 min (data/AI product design)
- Round 3: Technical - Engineer - 45-60 min
- Round 4: Culture / leadership - 45 min

## Question Bank
### Product Sense / Design
- "Design a data labeling product for autonomous vehicle companies." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you improve Scale's data annotation platform?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design a product for Scale to serve the generative AI fine-tuning market." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Technical
- "What is data labeling and why is it important for AI?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How do you ensure data quality at scale?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Explain RLHF and Scale's role in it." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

## What They Screen For
- AI/ML data fluency: understanding of training data, labeling, and data quality (source: Glassdoor)
- Enterprise/government sales sense: many clients are defense/government (source: Glassdoor)
- Technical depth: data infrastructure and ML pipelines (source: Glassdoor)
- Ambition and execution speed: startup culture with high expectations (source: Blind)

## Tips
- Scale's business has expanded from data labeling to AI infrastructure, evals, and RLHF. Know the full product suite. (source: Glassdoor)
- Government/defense work is a significant revenue stream. Be comfortable with this domain. (source: Blind)
- Understand how Scale fits into the AI value chain: training data, evaluation, fine-tuning, and deployment. (source: Glassdoor)
