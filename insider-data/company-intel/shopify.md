# Shopify - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: High

## Company Overview
- Stage: Public (SHOP)
- PM team size estimate: 300-500 PMs across Core Commerce, Payments, POS, Markets (International), and AI
- PM comp range: Senior PM $220K-$380K TC | Staff PM $350K-$520K TC | Director $480K-$700K+ TC
- Culture notes: "Make commerce better for everyone." Tobi Lutke (CEO) is deeply technical and product-minded. Remote-first ("Digital by Design"). Strong builder culture. Recently refocused after layoffs on AI and core commerce.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Round 0: Recruiter screen - 30 min
- Round 1: Hiring Manager screen - 45-60 min
- Round 2: Product craft case - 60 min (Life Story interview is unique to Shopify)
- Round 3: Technical / analytical - 45-60 min
- Round 4: GSD (Get Sh*t Done) interview - 45 min

## Question Bank
### Behavioral
- "Tell your life story and how it led you to product management." (frequency: very common, source: Glassdoor -- Shopify's signature)
- "Tell me about a time you shipped something scrappy." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Why commerce? Why Shopify?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Product Sense / Design
- "How would you improve Shopify's merchant onboarding?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design a feature to help Shopify merchants sell internationally." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you design AI features for Shopify merchants?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Execution / Metrics
- "What metrics matter for Shopify?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Shopify merchant churn increased. How do you investigate?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

## What They Screen For
- Merchant empathy: deep understanding of small business owners (source: Glassdoor)
- Builder mindset: scrappy, hands-on, ships fast (source: Glassdoor)
- Commerce knowledge: understanding of e-commerce, payments, logistics (source: Glassdoor)
- Craft: Tobi values product craft highly (source: Blind)

## Tips
- Shopify's "Life Story" interview is unique. Prepare a compelling narrative of your career journey. (source: Glassdoor)
- Know the Shopify ecosystem: themes, apps, Shopify Plus, POS, Markets. (source: Glassdoor)
- Tobi's memo on AI ("Shopify is an AI company") signals strategic direction. Read it. (source: Blind)
- Shopify competes with BigCommerce, WooCommerce, Amazon, and Squarespace. Know the landscape. (source: Glassdoor)
