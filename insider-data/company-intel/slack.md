# Slack (Salesforce) - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium-High

## Company Overview
- Stage: Salesforce subsidiary (acquired 2021 for $27.7B)
- PM team size estimate: 100-200 PMs across Core Messaging, Workflow Builder, Platform, AI, and Enterprise
- PM comp range: Senior PM $230K-$380K TC | Staff PM $340K-$520K TC (Salesforce comp structure)
- Culture notes: Collaborative, empathy-driven product culture within Salesforce. "Where work happens." Integration with Salesforce ecosystem is a key focus. SF-based.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5 (follows Salesforce process)
- Timeline: 3-6 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, behavioral/values, VP interview

## Question Bank
### Product Sense / Design
- "How would you improve Slack for enterprise companies?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design an AI assistant for Slack." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you improve Slack's channel discovery?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Execution / Metrics
- "What metrics matter for a workplace messaging platform?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Slack DAU is growing but messages per user is declining. Diagnose." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

## What They Screen For
- Collaboration product sense (source: Glassdoor)
- Enterprise workflow understanding (source: Glassdoor)
- Platform/ecosystem thinking (source: Glassdoor)
- Salesforce values alignment (source: Glassdoor)

## Tips
- Slack within Salesforce means integration is key. Know how Slack connects to Salesforce CRM. (source: Glassdoor)
- Slack competes with Microsoft Teams (primary competitor), Discord, and Google Chat. (source: Glassdoor)
- Slack AI and workflow automation are strategic priorities. (source: Glassdoor)
