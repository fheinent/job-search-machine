Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind

## Company Overview
- Stage: Public (SNAP)
- Product focus: Camera/messaging platform (Snapchat)
- PM team size estimate: 100-200
- PM comp range: Senior PM $250K-$420K TC / Staff PM $380K-$580K TC
- Culture notes: Camera company. LA-based. Young user demographic. AR/AI innovation leader. Evan Spiegel (CEO) is very design-focused.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, analytical/execution, behavioral/leadership
