# Twilio - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium-High

## Company Overview
- Stage: Public (TWLO)
- PM team size estimate: 150-250 PMs across Messaging, Voice, Email (SendGrid), Segment, and Flex
- PM comp range: Senior PM $220K-$360K TC | Staff PM $320K-$500K TC | Director $450K-$650K+ TC
- Culture notes: Developer-first API company. "Ask your developer" ethos. Jeff Lawson founded it; new CEO Khozema Shipchandler focuses on profitability. Strong engineering culture with emphasis on developer experience.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, technical/analytical, behavioral

## Question Bank
### Product Sense / Design
- "How would you improve Twilio's onboarding for new developers?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Design a communication product for healthcare." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "How would you integrate AI into Twilio's platform?" (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

### Technical
- "How do APIs work? Design an API for a notification system." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)
- "Explain CPaaS (Communications Platform as a Service)." (frequency: common, source: Glassdoor)

## What They Screen For
- Developer empathy: building for developers (source: Glassdoor)
- API/platform thinking (source: Glassdoor)
- B2B product sense (source: Glassdoor)
- Technical credibility (source: Glassdoor)

## Tips
- Know the CPaaS market: Twilio, Vonage, Bandwidth, MessageBird. (source: Glassdoor)
- Understand the Segment acquisition and how it fits into Twilio's customer data strategy. (source: Glassdoor)
- Twilio is refocusing on profitability after growth-at-all-costs. Show P&L awareness. (source: Blind)
