Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind

## Company Overview
- Stage: Late-stage private (~$4B)
- Product focus: Visual web development
- PM team size estimate: 30-50
- PM comp range: Senior PM $220K-$370K TC / Staff PM $330K-$500K TC
- Culture notes: Visual web development platform. SF-based. No-code website builder for designers and developers. Strong community.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
