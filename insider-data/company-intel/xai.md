## Company Overview
- Stage: Late-stage private (valued at $50B+ as of early 2026)
- PM team size estimate: 20-50 PMs across Grok, API, and Enterprise

- Total rounds: 3-5
- Timeline: 1-3 weeks (fast)

- Round 3: Culture / intensity fit - 45 min

- Note: Process is fast and may be unconventional (Musk-style)
