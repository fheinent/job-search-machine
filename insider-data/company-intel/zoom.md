# Zoom - PM Interview Intel
Last updated: 2026-03-24
Sources: Glassdoor, levels.fyi, Blind
Confidence: Medium-High

## Company Overview
- Stage: Public (ZM)
- PM team size estimate: 150-250 PMs across Meetings, Phone, Contact Center, Workvivo, and AI Companion
- PM comp range: Senior PM $220K-$370K TC | Staff PM $320K-$500K TC | Director $450K-$650K+ TC
- Culture notes: "Delivering Happiness" culture. Eric Yuan (CEO) is customer-obsessed. Post-pandemic, Zoom is expanding beyond video into a full workplace platform. San Jose HQ.

## Interview Format
- Total rounds: 4-5
- Timeline: 3-5 weeks
- Typical: Recruiter screen, HM screen, product case, analytical, behavioral
